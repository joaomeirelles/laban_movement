Joao Meirelles

I am a Phd student at HERUS / EPFL. I am also part of DATABASE / Rio de Janero and of Metabolism of Cities.

Prior to that, I had worked as a Data Scientist at PENSA, the big data team for the city of Rio de Janeiro.

I hold a Bsc in Environmental and Water Resources Engineering from UFF and a Msc in Complex Systems Modeling from USP.

My research interest orbits around data science, complex systems and cities, mainly focused on the urban metabolism.
